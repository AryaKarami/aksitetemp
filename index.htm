About

I am an experienced data scientist focused on the theoretical development of statistical network modelling.

With a network-oriented mindset and a strong appreciation for the multidisciplinary nature of networks, I have planned, executed, and led multiple interdisciplinary projects.

I aim to enhance the functionality and performance of complex socio-X systems by uncovering patterns in individual and group behaviour. Explore my portfolio, which features projects highlighting my proficiency in navigating the intersection of statistical modelling and AI applications to support decision-making across industries.

Should you find these subjects of interest, I would be delighted to discuss them further.

For collaborations or speaking engagements, please feel free to reach out at arya.karami@brunel.ac.uk.
